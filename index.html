Works offline after download. Best sent on WhatsApp as a document attachment.

How it works

The quiz tracks six scores and also tracks how many different questions contributed to each bird. So yes, the spreadsheet has a personality.

The six birds

Hawk: price stability and credibility. Dove: employment and output stabilization. Pigeon: prudence and data dependence. Eagle: structural change and long-run vision. Parrot: holistic policy context. Crow: crisis creativity and unconventional tools.

Classification logic

Crow, Parrot, Eagle, and Pigeon are checked first using combinations of score rank and repeated answer patterns. Hawk and Dove are separated at the end by their relative balance, because apparently even birds need an identification strategy.
